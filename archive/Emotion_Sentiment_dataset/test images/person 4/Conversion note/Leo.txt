Video Call Conversation: The Return Match
Setting: A video call between Leo, a 29-year-old wrestler, and his friend/trainer.
(Call connects. Leo's face is initially Neutral.)
Trainer: Yo, Leo. 'Bout time ya picked up.
Leo: (His expression shifts to a slight frown of Contempt.) Yea, I know, I know. Was busy. What's up?
Trainer: What's up? You see the news?
Leo: (His face tightens with Anger.) Don't tell me... they changed the venue again. I swear to god, if they moved it...
Trainer: Nah, man, not the venue. Worse. They've changed your opponent.
Leo: (His face drops, a look of Disgust crosses his features.) Ugh. Who'd they put me up against? Not that new kid. The one with the weak armbar.
Trainer: Nah, not him. It's The Butcher.
Leo: (His eyes widen slightly, a flash of Fear.) The Butcher?! No... are you serious?
Trainer: Dead serious. His last match was brutal. They're sayin' he's in peak condition.
Leo: (A genuine Happy smile breaks across his face.) Oh, man! This is perfect!
Trainer: (Confused) Perfect?! You were just freaking out!
Leo: Nah, nah, man, listen. They think he's gonna tear me up. They think I'm the underdog. I love it! I've been training for this! This is the match I wanted!
Trainer: (Chuckles) Okay, okay. I get it. The bigger the challenge...
Leo: (A sudden change to a Sad expression, his shoulders slump slightly.) I just... I gotta make sure I'm ready. My leg's been actin' up since the last bout. I can't let him exploit that.
Trainer: We'll work on it. We'll be ready. Hey... you got this.
Leo: (His eyes go wide, a look of pure Surprise.) They just posted the promo vid! Man, they're not even tryin' to hide it! They're callin' me the "sacrificial lamb"!
Trainer: Let 'em. It'll make your win that much sweeter.
(Leo's face returns to Neutral, a determined look in his eyes as he nods slowly.)
Leo: You're right. You're absolutely right. Thanks, man. I needed to hear that. See you at the gym.

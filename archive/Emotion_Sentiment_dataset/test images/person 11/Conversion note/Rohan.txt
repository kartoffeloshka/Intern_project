Characters:
* Rohan (33): The host of the party, friendly and a little stressed.
* Aditya (33): Rohan's childhood friend, laid-back and observant.

A Party Conversation
Rohan: (Wiping his forehead, holding a drink) Hey man, so glad you could make it! I know it’s been a while.
Aditya: (Smiling, looking around) No way I'd miss it, especially not for your 33rd. This place is packed! You really pulled it off. Happy birthday, by the way.
Rohan: Thanks, but this is less a birthday party and more a 'let's just get together before we all turn 40 and forget how to have fun' party. I think I've seen more people tonight than in the last six months combined.
Aditya: (Chuckles) Sounds about right. So, how's everything? How's the new job? You said you were working on some big project.
Rohan: It's... intense. We're launching a new app, and the pressure is insane. Long nights, short weekends. But it's also exhilarating. It's the kind of work you know is going to be a game-changer. I just wish I had more time to breathe.
Aditya: I get that. The grind is real. You've always been a workhorse. Just make sure you're not burning out. You look a little... wired.
Rohan: (Laughs) Wired is an understatement. I've been running on coffee and a sheer will to not mess this up. But what about you? Still with the same company? Last I heard, you were thinking about taking a sabbatical to travel.
Aditya: Yeah, still there. But the travel plan is still on my mind. I've been saving up. Might do a trip through Southeast Asia next year. Solo trip, you know? Just for a few months, no schedule, no deadlines. Just me and a backpack.
Rohan: (Shakes his head in admiration) You’re brave, man. I could never do that. My calendar is my life. But that sounds amazing.
Aditya: It's less about being brave and more about needing a break. A real one. So, you think you’ll ever get a chance to take a breather from work?
Rohan: Maybe. One day. (He smiles) But for now, let's just enjoy the party. I've got a killer playlist going in the living room. Come on, let's grab another round.
Aditya: Lead the way, birthday boy.
